Sunday Night Live: Peterston

Sunday 22nd June, 7.30pm, town hall free entry

Learn more

What is it?

Sunday Night Live is a free event at Peterston Super Ely town hall aimed at 11-16 year olds.

We're going to have live music, space to chill, and good food and soft drinks. Should be a lot of fun and wont' cost you a penny!

We will have a beatbox workshop with Make Some Noise UK battle champion BeatMuppet, who will teach you the basics. If you're feeling brave, there will be a beatbox battle, and the winner will take home a sweet trophy!
