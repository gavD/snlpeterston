Sunday Night Live: Peterston

Sunday 22nd June, 7.30pm, community hall - free live music event!

Learn more

What is it?

Sunday Night Live is a free event at Peterston Super Ely Community Hall aimed at 11-16 year olds.

We're going to have live music, space to chill, and good food and soft drinks. Should be a lot of fun and won't cost you a penny!

The fantastic recording artist Sophie de Winter will be doing a live set - you can check her out on Spotify or loudr.fm! We also have a rock band playing a diverse set of covers.

We will have a beatbox workshop with Make Some Noise UK battle champion BeatMuppet, who will teach you the basics. If you're feeling brave, there will be a beatbox battle, and the winner will take home this sweet trophy!

No need to book - just drop in! Do feel free to join the Facebook group, though!
